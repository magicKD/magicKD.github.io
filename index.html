Rainfall Algorithm: A novel nature-inspired meta-heuristic optimization algorithm
I independently designed and proposed this heuristic algorithm in my sophomore year inspired by the rainy climate in Guangzhou, China. I was surprised to find that this heuristic algorithm can achieve high performance in solving the Steiner tree problem (NP-complete problem). Therefore, I plan to investigate the performance of this algorithm and provide some theoretical analysis.
